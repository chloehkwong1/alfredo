A — Dropdown + Chips

Compact trigger shows selected repos as colored chips. Click to open multi-select list with checkboxes. Full repo names visible when open. Collapses to one line when closed.

B — Toggle Pills

Like current pills but click toggles on/off instead of switching. Active pills are colored, inactive are dimmed. Wraps when needed. Always visible — no dropdown to open. Each repo gets a unique color.

C — Collapsible Filter

Header shows "2 repos" badge. Click it to expand the repo filter below the header. Toggle pills on/off. Collapse when done. Zero space when you don't need it, full control when you do.
